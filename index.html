Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Flexmix app ("the App") handles your information. The App is built and published by HEYIU117 ("we", "us", "our"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

Summary

Flexmix does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information stays on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you. The App does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or any other identifying information.

Information Stored on Your Device

To make the App work, Flexmix saves a small amount of preference data locally on your device using Apple's standard storage system (UserDefaults). This may include:

  • Your selected workout schedule
  • Your preferred number of exercises per session
  • Your current tier selection (Core or Boost)

This data never leaves your device. We do not have access to it. If you delete the App, this data is removed with it.

Network Usage

The App does not connect to any servers operated by us or by third parties. It does not send analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or any other data over the internet.

Third-Party Services

Flexmix does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, the App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction).

Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no data on our side for you to access, correct, or delete. You can clear all locally stored preferences at any time by deleting the App from your device.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
